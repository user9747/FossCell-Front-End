-About-

Free and open-source software (FOSS) cell is a group of students passionate and enthusiastic about free software and technology. Our vision is to bring more student developers into the open-source community and promote open-source mindset in the campus. The cell frequently conducts coding contests and sessions on open-source software. We provide mentorship for student projects.
